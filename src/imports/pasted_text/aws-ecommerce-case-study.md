Building a Scalable Multi-Brand Ecommerce Platform on AWS for a Leading Retail Group in Southeast Asia

Executive Summary
A leading multi-brand retail chain headquartered in Indonesia partnered with Nabhira Technologies to modernize its digital commerce ecosystem and expand its presence across Southeast Asia. The company manages and markets several international fashion and apparel brands across markets including Indonesia, Vietnam, Philippines, Cambodia, and Singapore.
The organization needed a scalable and resilient digital platform capable of supporting multiple brand storefronts, omnichannel retail operations, and integrated supply chain management. Their legacy on-premise infrastructure limited scalability, operational efficiency, and speed of innovation.
Nabhira Technologies designed and implemented a cloud-native ecommerce and retail platform on AWS, migrating legacy systems from on-premise infrastructure to the cloud. The solution included multi-brand ecommerce portals, store management applications, and supply chain integration systems that support regional growth and high-volume digital commerce.
By leveraging the AWS cloud platform, the retailer achieved improved performance, faster deployment cycles, enhanced scalability during peak seasons, and significantly reduced infrastructure management overhead.

Customer Background
The customer is a leading Indonesian retail conglomerate specializing in fashion and apparel distribution for globally recognized brands. The company operates both physical retail outlets and digital commerce platforms across Southeast Asia.
With growing consumer demand for online retail and omnichannel experiences, the organization sought to strengthen its digital capabilities and create a unified platform capable of managing:
Multi-brand ecommerce storefronts


Inventory and supply chain operations


Store management and retail analytics


Cross-border commerce across multiple Southeast Asian markets


The company’s expansion strategy required a modern, scalable digital infrastructure capable of supporting regional growth and large transaction volumes.
Problem / Challenge
The retailer faced several technology and operational challenges due to its legacy systems:
1. Legacy On-Premise Infrastructure
The existing ecommerce and retail systems were hosted on on-premise data centers, which created challenges in scalability, maintenance, and operational costs.
2. Limited Scalability During Peak Sales
Seasonal campaigns, promotional events, and brand launches caused traffic spikes, resulting in system performance degradation and slower customer experiences.
3. Multi-Country Expansion Complexity
Expanding ecommerce operations across Southeast Asian markets required a platform capable of supporting multi-currency, localization, and regional logistics integrations.
4. Fragmented Retail and Supply Chain Systems
Existing systems for store operations, ecommerce, and supply chain management operated in silos, limiting real-time visibility into inventory and fulfillment.
5. Slow Deployment and Innovation Cycles
Infrastructure provisioning and software deployments required manual intervention, slowing down the launch of new features and brands.


Solution / Implementation
Nabhira Technologies designed and implemented a cloud-first retail technology platform leveraging AWS cloud services, enabling the retailer to modernize its digital commerce ecosystem and support regional expansion.
Cloud Migration Strategy
The project began with a structured migration of applications from on-premise infrastructure to AWS, ensuring minimal disruption to existing retail operations.
Key steps included:
Assessment of legacy systems and application architecture


Cloud readiness evaluation


Migration planning and phased deployment strategy


Data migration and system modernization



AWS Cloud Architecture
The new platform was built using AWS cloud infrastructure to ensure high availability, scalability, and operational efficiency.
Key AWS components included:
Amazon EC2 for scalable application hosting


Amazon RDS for managed relational database services


Amazon S3 for secure and scalable storage of product images and digital assets


Amazon CloudFront for global content delivery and improved customer experience


AWS Auto Scaling to handle peak traffic during promotional campaigns


AWS Load Balancer for application reliability and high availability


AWS CloudWatch for monitoring and operational visibility


Multi-Brand Ecommerce Platform
Nabhira Technologies developed a centralized ecommerce platform supporting multiple international fashion brands.
Key capabilities include:
Multi-brand storefront architecture


Regional localization support


Mobile-optimized ecommerce experiences


Secure payment integrations


Product catalog and promotion management


Store and Retail Applications
The solution also included retail store management applications enabling seamless coordination between physical stores and digital commerce platforms.
Capabilities include:
Store inventory synchronization


Retail sales tracking


Omnichannel order management


Real-time inventory visibility


Supply Chain Integration
To improve operational efficiency, Nabhira integrated supply chain management modules into the platform.
Key capabilities:
Warehouse inventory management


Order fulfillment optimization


Logistics integration for regional delivery partners


Inventory planning and demand forecasting

Through its partnership with Nabhira Technologies, the Indonesian retail group successfully transformed its digital commerce infrastructure by migrating from legacy on-premise systems to a modern AWS-powered cloud platform.
