Building an Integrated POS and Hyperlocal Delivery Platform on AWS for a Large Fresh Food Retail Chain

Executive Summary
A leading fresh food and vegetable hyperlocal retail chain in South India partnered with Nabhira Technologies to rapidly launch a digital commerce platform capable of delivering fresh groceries to customers within 30 minutes. With an extensive network of 750 franchise stores and an average of 9,000 orders per day, the retailer required a highly scalable and reliable technology platform to support real-time inventory management, fast order processing, and hyperlocal logistics.
To meet aggressive timelines, Nabhira Technologies adopted a cloud-native development approach leveraging AWS cloud services to design and deploy an integrated retail platform within three months. The solution included a Store POS system, Ecommerce portal, Order Management system, and Supply Chain platform, enabling seamless coordination between online ordering, in-store operations, and delivery logistics.
The AWS-powered platform now supports thousands of daily transactions, enabling the retailer to expand digital commerce while maintaining operational efficiency across its franchise network.

Customer Background
The customer is a large fresh food and vegetable retail chain operating across South India, with a strong presence through franchise-based neighborhood stores. The company specializes in delivering fresh produce, groceries, and daily essentials to local communities.
With the rapid growth of online grocery ordering and hyperlocal delivery services, the retailer recognized the need to modernize its operations and offer customers a seamless digital ordering experience with fast delivery times.
The organization operates approximately 750 franchise retail stores, which serve as local fulfillment centers for hyperlocal deliveries. To support digital expansion and ensure consistent service quality, the company sought a technology partner capable of delivering a robust and scalable retail platform within a short timeframe.

Problem / Challenge
The retailer faced several key challenges while attempting to transition to an online hyperlocal delivery model.
1. Rapid Digital Launch Requirement
The company needed to launch a fully functional ecommerce and delivery platform within three months, enabling customers to order groceries online and receive deliveries within 30 minutes.

2. Large Distributed Store Network
With 750 franchise stores, the retailer required a system capable of managing store-level inventory, order routing, and delivery logistics across multiple cities.

3. Real-Time Inventory Synchronization
Fresh food retail requires real-time stock visibility, as inventory changes frequently due to perishability and daily replenishment cycles.

4. High Order Volume
The platform needed to support approximately 9,000 daily orders, with the ability to scale during peak demand periods such as weekends and promotions.

5. Integrated Retail Operations
Existing operations relied on fragmented systems for:
Store billing


Inventory management


Customer ordering


Supply chain coordination


The retailer needed a unified technology platform that connected these functions seamlessly.

Solution / Implementation
Nabhira Technologies designed and implemented a cloud-native hyperlocal retail platform using AWS, enabling rapid development, deployment, and scalability.
The solution was architected as a modular retail ecosystem, integrating ecommerce, store operations, order processing, and supply chain management.

Cloud-Native Architecture on AWS
The platform was built using AWS cloud services, ensuring high availability, performance, and scalability.
Key AWS services used included:
Amazon EC2 for scalable application hosting


Amazon RDS for secure and managed relational databases


Amazon S3 for storing product images and digital assets


Amazon CloudFront for fast content delivery


AWS Auto Scaling for handling peak order volumes


Elastic Load Balancer for high availability and traffic distribution


AWS CloudWatch for monitoring and operational insights


This cloud-native architecture allowed the platform to scale automatically based on demand, ensuring uninterrupted operations across hundreds of franchise stores.

Store POS System
A modern cloud-connected POS system was developed for franchise stores to support daily retail operations.
Key capabilities include:
Real-time billing and checkout


Inventory updates across stores


Integration with ecommerce orders


Store-level reporting and analytics


The POS system ensures that store inventory and online availability remain synchronized.

Ecommerce Portal
A mobile-friendly ecommerce platform was developed to allow customers to browse products, place orders, and schedule deliveries.
Features include:
Product catalog management


Online ordering and payment integration


Customer account management


Order tracking and notifications


Customers can easily order fresh produce and groceries and receive deliveries within 30 minutes.

Order Management System
The Order Management System (OMS) coordinates orders across the retail network.
Capabilities include:
Smart order routing to the nearest franchise store


Real-time order status tracking


Delivery assignment and dispatch management


Order lifecycle monitoring


This system ensures fast order fulfillment and efficient delivery coordination.

Supply Chain and Inventory Management
Nabhira implemented a centralized supply chain system to support store replenishment and demand forecasting.
Capabilities include:
Warehouse inventory visibility


Store-level stock planning


Automated replenishment workflows


Demand forecasting for fresh produce


This helps the retailer maintain optimal stock levels and reduce waste.
